ABOUT XININKS

Rooted in Caribbean Culture, Driven by Global Vision

Nicolin Harris — CEO & Owner. BA Visual Arts (Specialization) from The University of the West Indies, with a focus in Fine Arts and a Minor in Cultural Studies, plus a Graphic Design certification from California Institute of the Arts.

XINinks was born out of a love for the ink medium — starting as an illustrative and body art practice before growing into a full creative and print consultancy, now serving clients across Trinidad & Tobago, Canada, and Switzerland.

PORTFOLIO / CASE FILES

Proof, Not Just Capability

Two production engagements that back up the operations claims above.

CASE FILE / 01 Commercial Client Work

Arnim's Art Gallery — The Frame Shop

Production & Framing Consultant, on staff

Delivered large-format signage and gallery display work as commercially invoiced client projects — substrate handling built for outdoor durability, full prepress file setup, and brand consistency held across every physical touch-point, from look-books and flyers through to final gallery signage.

Framing consultancy went beyond production: functioning as quality assurance on every piece — structural design decisions, material logistics and sourcing, and managing client expectations from drop-off through to final presentation.

Produced exhibition and event posters for artists including 1ndividual and Muhammad Muwakil, and translated original watercolour paintings into print-ready cards and canvases for seasonal Christmas market runs.

Outdoor-Durable Substrates Prepress File Setup Structural Framing QA Client Presentation Management
CASE FILE / 02 Editorial Production Management

Local Colouring Book

Production Manager & Editor

Managed the book from manuscript to press — editing content, running pre-flight file checks, selecting paper stock, and holding registration and margins steady across the full run, then overseeing physical print production through to completion.
